Indian Railway Safety Measures

  • Do not board or deboard moving trains.
  • Stand behind the yellow line on platforms.
  • Use footbridges or subways to cross tracks.
  • Do not carry flammable items on trains.
  • Stay alert while using earphones near platforms.
  • Do not climb on goods wagons or locomotives.
  • Ensure children are supervised at all times.
  • Avoid overcrowding at station entry points.

Helpline Numbers:

General: 139 | Emergency: 112 | Women Helpline: 182
